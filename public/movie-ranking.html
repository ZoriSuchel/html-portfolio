Best Films of All Time

My top 3 films of all-time

Amélie

French Drama 2001

Despite being caught in her imaginative world, Amelie, a young waitress, decides to help people find happiness.

Nausicaa

Japanese, Ghibli Studio-1984

Warrior and pacifist Princess Nausicaä desperately struggles to prevent two warring nations from destroying themselves and their dying planet.

Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith

USA, scifi, 2005

Three years into the Clone Wars, Obi-Wan Kenobi pursues a new threat, while Anakin Skywalker is lured by Chancellor Palpatine into a sinister plot to rule the galaxy.
